VIBRATIONAL THERAPY

Vibrational Therapy is a technique of energetic treatment through thought and structured intention that uses commands to achieve a certain objective in the physical, emotional, mental and spiritual body. Our mind needs intention and repetition to generate the energy charge necessary to modify an installed pattern, this can be an emotion, trauma or physical illness. It is necessary to find the point of origin that is generating and maintaining this emotion, and that is repeating the behavior to each same or similar event. When the point of origin is found, the energetic transformation is carried out to transmute what sustains that emotion. With this, a new positive point of origin begins to sustain that situation, allowing the person to have a positive behavior in relation to that event or any other that activates the same emotion. The session consists of a brief conversation to understand the situation, followed by a guided meditation to facilitate access to the point of origin of the emotion.
