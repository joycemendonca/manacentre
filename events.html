We know there are a lot of you experiencing financial hardship but also in immense need of healing. We have heard you! ♥

As a response to your calling and a way we can attend to our own will of serving, we are thrilled to be launching the SOCIAL HEALING SESSIONS!

This is a CHARITABLE SERVICE where we offer HOLISTIC THERAPIES for anyone who is seeking healing.

Our expert team is highly qualified to support you with: APOMETRY and REIKI. (This is not a Tarot service.)

The SOCIAL HEALING SESSIONS are happening MONTHLY in BERLIN.
